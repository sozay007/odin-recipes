Scrambled-Eggs

Description

How to make light and fluffy scrambled-eggs for quick and easy breakfast to start your day.To do this, follow the technique in this recipe for perfect results everytime.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cup of milk
  • Salt to taste

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Get all the ingredients ready.

    2. Step 2

  2. Break the eggs into a microwave-proof mixing bowl. Add milk and salt; mix well.

    3. Step 3

  3. Put the bowl into the microwave and cook on high power for 30 seconds. Remove bowl, beat eggs very well, scraping down the sides of the bowl, and return to the microwave for another 30 seconds.

    4. Step 4

  4. Repeat this process stirring every 30 seconds for up to 2 1/2 minutes. Stop when eggs have the consistency you desire.

    5. Step 5

  5. Serve and enjoy!
